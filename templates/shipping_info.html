Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package in several ways:

  • Visit our Track Order page and enter your tracking number
  • Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Track directly on the carrier's website (DHL, UPS, FedEx, USPS)

You'll also receive SMS updates if you provided a mobile number at checkout.

If you're not home, the carrier will typically:

  • Leave a delivery attempt notice with instructions
  • Leave the package in a safe place (at driver's discretion for non-signature items)
  • Deliver to a neighbor (with your prior authorization)

For valuable items requiring signature, you can arrange for redelivery or pick up from their local facility. Most carriers will make 3 delivery attempts before returning to sender.

Yes, we ship to PO boxes via standard shipping (USPS, Royal Mail, La Poste). However, please note:

  • Express shipping options (FedEx, UPS, DHL) cannot deliver to PO boxes
  • Some international destinations may restrict PO box delivery
  • Signature-required items cannot be delivered to PO boxes

If you select express shipping with a PO box, we'll contact you to arrange alternative delivery.

You can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order:

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the order and click "Edit Shipping Address"
  3. Contact our customer service immediately at +237 650-970-291

After the 2-hour window, changes may not be possible as orders begin processing. If your order has already shipped, we cannot redirect it.

Our shipping cut-off times are as follows:

Standard Shipping: Orders before 4 PM ship same day
Express Shipping: Orders before 2 PM ship same day for next-day delivery
International: Orders before 12 PM ship same day

Orders placed after cut-off or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.
